How Do I Get Ready For My First Visit ?

At Peak Performance, preparation is key. Here are a few tips to get you ready for your first visit.

  • First, contact the one of our three locations that suits you best.
  • Bring your filled out intake forms, physical therapy prescrition, ID and insurance card
  • Please wear loose fitting clothes
  • Be aware that the injured area will need to be exposed during the evaluation

What To Expect ?

After your evaluation, your physical therapist will design an individualized treatment plan to return you to a pain-free, independent lifestyle.

If you do not understand your diagnosis, your physical therapist will ensure that you understand the context of your treatment with a thorough explanation.

You will meet with your physical therapist three times a week for four weeksto maximize recovery before you return to your physician.

Each session will last between one and two hours

When you are finished with your therapy from Peak Performance, you should expect to return to your prior pain-free functional level and have an excellent grasp of your personalized exercises.
